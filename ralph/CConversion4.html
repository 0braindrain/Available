How the Cosmic Units Converter Works

This converter maintains a constant relationship between base weight and total weight:

Total Weight = Base Weight + 0.314 g

Base Weight: The weight of the primary material

Total Weight: Base weight plus a constant plastic component of 0.314g

Volume: Cubic centimeters (cc) measurement of the base material
